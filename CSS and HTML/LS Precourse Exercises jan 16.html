Pear and Bacon Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

2 tablespoons of unsalted butter

2 tablespoons of currant or fig jam

2 slices white sandwitch bread

2 slices Cheddar

2 slices cooked bacon

1/4 small pear, thinly sliced

Directions

1. Melt the butter in a small skillet over medium-low heat. Spread the jam on 1 slice of the bread and form a sandwitch with the cheese, bacon, and pear.

2. Cook the sandwitch, covered, until the bread is toasted and the cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes per side.
